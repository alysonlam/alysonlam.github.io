Introduction

Science Olympiad is one of the largest academic clubs at Mason High School, located in Southwest Ohio. Together, we compete in 23 different events covering a wide variety of scientific topics. Our team has been established since 2003 and over the years have constantly reached new heights, recently qualifying for the National Tourament to be held at Colorado State University in Fort Collins for the first time in our history. As champions, we also break a 20 year Ohio Science Olympiad record.

Our Team

We are a collection of students at MHS with a passion for science beyond the classroom. For hours on end, we seek to break barriers and expectations by working hard and pursuing our interests. In the few short years of high school, the time we've spent together has united us as one family.

2018 States

Our Mission

For the past 4 years, our goal has remained the same: get to Nationals. Now that we've finally fulfilled that, our mission is to now stand together on that National stage, and excel.

My Role

As a member since my freshman year and a captain in my junior and senior year, I have seen the success of this team over the last couple of years and have played a part as both a competitor and leader.

In addition to the responsiblities I hold as captain, I have also overseen the design of both spiritwear and all other graphic materials ranging from posters, to large scale banners, and websites. The most significant of my work being our spiritwear.
